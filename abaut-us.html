PRIVATE BRUSH TEAM

Let us introduce ourselves to you

Michał "Ciosu" Cios

Founder of the studio, student of management, painter for more than 9 years. Painted almost all the models for By Fire and Sword game, uncountable numbers of bases, main painter of the Wargamer Company. He loves 15mm models, just can't resist to paint them- especially II World War Flames of War. He is also collecting 1:1 - 12th SS Division "Hitlerjugend"- he is a total maniaque! It is a massive project.

Mateusz "Mateo" Piasecki

Student of archeology, his favorite period is antiquity and he loves 15mm miniatures (especially By Fire and Sword), but he is not afraid of larger scales.

Łukasz "Luigi" Piasecki

A twin brother of Mateusz, also a student of archeology. He loves larger scales, but is also a great 15mm painter. He is our "news" guy from the miniature world.

Paweł Oleszczuk

Historian, sculptor and handyman in our studio- there is nothing impossible for him in making, converting, painting miniatures. He just can do everything, trust us.
