Assembling

First thing what we do when we get your models. We cut, clean mold lines, fill holes assembly your models and prepare them for painting. A good prepared model, just awaits to be painted.

Painting

The most important part of our job. We basecoat your models, paint them with airbrush and brush techniques- we just pure life in those plastic, resin or metal models.

Basing

Basing is this what gives our painted models character. We do bases, depending from the army and model character. Most models have simple base, but if you want your army to have special, unique look- just give us a try.
